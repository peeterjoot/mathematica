My Mathematica notebooks for 'Exploring physics with Geometric Algebra'.

This page lists my mathematica notebooks for 'Exploring physics with Geometric Algebra'. The most recent copy of any of these notebooks can all be obtained from my github Mathematica repository.

If you do not have a copy of Mathematica, many of these notebooks can be viewed with the free Wolfram CDF player.

Chronological listing of Mathematica notebooks

  • Sep 15, 2011 gabook/pendulumDouble.nb

  • May 4, 2014 gabook/sphericalSurfaceAndVolumeElements.nb

    In a 4D Euclidean space, this notebook calculates the spherical tangent space basis for a spherical parameterization of span e2,e3,e4 and their duals on that volume. The duals are calculated using the Geometric algebra methods, instead of matrix inversion. This notebook uses clifford.m
